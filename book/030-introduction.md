# Introduction: Reverse Engineering Life

Every complex system can be understood by observation, modeling and experimentation. This book applies the same mindset to the most fascinating system that I’ve ever encountered: human life.

I hope to consider this work as somewhat scientific research in the field of _metaphysics_. Having studied regular physics in university, I remember the feeling of awe when I first saw how classical mechanics can be deduced logically from a very small set of assumptions - the three laws of Newton.

I would also want to clarify that I’m not particularly knowledgeable in the field of spirituality. If I misuse common terminology or draw incorrect parallels, I apologize in advance.
