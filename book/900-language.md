# Language of the Pure Souls

I decided to write this book in a very special language that I recently discovered. With lack of a better name, I’ll refer to it as the _language of the pure souls_.

This is the language that children speak to themselves before our corrupted society ruins them with our lower frequencies.

This is also the language spoken in the “divine” realm above us where souls have graduated this earth and have become pure and full of love. Enlightened.

It’s a fascinating language that we can also speak to people around us. But more importantly, it’s a language that we can speak to ourselves. Our own internal voice. I believe that speaking this language is the fastest way to progress in your own self-work/spiritual journey.

This is how this language works:

You speak from the perspective of a pure soul-mind inhabiting a human body. A different perspective from the commonly used body-mind.

You speak with the bewilderment of a pure innocent child full of genuine love that sees the world for the first time and you make pure observations without any bias and without any constraints.

Then you take these observations and analyze them with the pure logic and precision of a machine - with mental models and math formulas. Maintaining the purest most innocent intentions and assuming everybody else is as amazingly loving as you are. You then make logical deductions based on that.

When you finish, you return to making new observations again until the mental model stabilizes and a truth emerges.
